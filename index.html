About

Weaving Connections with
the Seen and Unseen

I grew up in the small towns and fields and forests of New Hampshire. The timeless time I spent wandering over stone walls, and resting by beaver ponds, allowed me to weave a deep connection with the natural world, and also with all that exists beyond what meets the naked eye.

Early on, I was drawn to connecting with presences that I felt, but could not see. I was consistently delighted by how they seemed to work through, and with, the natural world. I became conscious of how a sudden flight of a hawk was directly related to the thought I had. I became aware of signs and symbols and synchronicities.

Alongside my deepening relationship with nature, I began attending the yearly dowsers convention, a gathering of people, mostly adults, who validated within my young mind the reality of connection with consciousness in all of life and in the unseen world. I was shown that the ways in which to communicate with all of life, are limitless.

Offerings

Sessions & Readings

Spirit Connection / Mediumship

Mediumship is the act of connecting with spirits who no longer are in body. It is something each one of us is capable of doing, yet we are not commonly made aware of this. There are a growing number of people who are opening to these skills, allowing more spirits to communicate with their loved ones.

What to Expect in a Session

Before we even start I will have set the intention to connect with a spirit who wishes to connect. My intention always includes that the session is in a container of love and what is in the best and highest good for you.

The session may include a visit from a loved one or multiple loved ones, including pets or spirits who act as guardians to you from the spirit world. Occasionally someone who is still in body may pop in to say hello as a spirit. This can be a delightful surprise as well.

Intuitive Art Card Readings
