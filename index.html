About

Weaving Connections with
the Seen and Unseen

I grew up in the small towns and fields and forests of New Hampshire. The timeless time I spent wandering over stone walls, and resting by beaver ponds, allowed me to weave a deep connection with the natural world, and also with all that exists beyond what meets the naked eye. Early on, I was drawn to connecting with presences that I felt, but could not see. I was consistently delighted by how they seemed to work through, and with, the natural world. I became conscious of how the sudden flight of a hawk was directly related to the thought I had just experienced. I became aware of signs and symbols and synchronicities.

Alongside my deepening relationship with nature, I began attending the yearly dowsers convention, a gathering of people, mostly adults, who worked with honoring their connection to their own innate intuition and also their relationship with the divine. I felt deep validation to experience adults taking all this wonder seriously. Within my young mind, the reality of connection with consciousness in all of life and in the unseen world was anchored in, and I never closed that door between the wonder and magic of childhood and the big world of adulthood.

As I matured, my journey led me to working with many different healers and teachers, and into a deep study of energy, creativity and of the depression that impacted my life for many years. As an Aquarius, my mind was active and inquisitive, so I was always studying my experience, even when it was very painful. I described it as an undercover mission to understand depression wherein I was so deeply involved that I often forgot I was undercover and I became lost in the abyss. :)

Subsequently, I became very intimate with many levels and aspects of depression and acquired much insight and wisdom on this topic, especially as it relates to the realm of the unseen world that I so cherish and am delighted by. I have come to a vast understanding of the power of CREATIVITY to heal one back from depression to feeling like themselves again. I offer tools and concepts and support in this area, and have a deep respect for those in the thick layers of this experience.

Creating Connections is an alchemical experience that begins with an inquiry and an intention. This sets in motion a conversation, with one's own spirit and whatever one considers to be the divine.

Through the experience of a session online, or in person, you will receive layers of information and imagery all informed by the inquiry you have set in motion. This session will continue to live and grow and reveal and heal, long after our time together.

Offerings

Sessions & Readings

Spirit Connection / Mediumship

Mediumship is the act of connecting with spirits who no longer are in body. It is something each one of us is capable of doing, yet we are not commonly made aware of this. There are a growing number of people who are opening to these skills, allowing more spirits to communicate with their loved ones.

What to Expect in a Session

Before we even start I will have set the intention to connect with a spirit who wishes to connect. My intention always includes that the session is in a container of love and what is in the best and highest good for you.

The session may include a visit from a loved one or multiple loved ones, including pets or spirits who act as guardians to you from the spirit world. Occasionally someone who is still in body may pop in to say hello as a spirit. This can be a delightful surprise as well.

Intuitive Art Card Readings

Art card readings utilize a growing deck of large cards I have created through collage. They read different for each person, which always fascinates me.

What to Expect

We will choose a card together and I will first bring in your input on what it is that you see and feel. Then I will read the card for you and you will receive a reading with multiple layers. I will use the card and what it brings up as a springboard for the intuitive reading, bringing in information and new perspectives to assist you on your path.

Depression Rebel Session

Connecting Self with Self

If you have exhausted all avenues to heal the depression that impacts you, and you are ready and open to explore something different, I would love to help. I am here to offer new angles and tools to assist you in feeling "like yourself" again. Please inquire if this draws you.
